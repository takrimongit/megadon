# AdForge AI — Video Ad Scripts

Two ready-to-shoot scripts: one long-form for YouTube, one fast-cut for an
Instagram/TikTok Reel. Both use real app footage (screen recordings) plus
voiceover or burned-in captions.

---

## 1. YouTube Ad — "From Brief to Batch" (~60s)

**Format:** screen-recording B-roll of the actual app + voiceover (VO).
Timestamps are guides, not hard marks.

| Time | Visual | Voiceover |
|------|--------|-----------|
| 0:00–0:04 | Hard cut: a marketer staring at a blank "Create Ad" canvas, clock ticking. | "Your next ad campaign needs forty creatives. By Friday." |
| 0:04–0:10 | Cut to AdForge dashboard sliding in, Deep Indigo → Purple gradient. | "Meet AdForge AI — the self-improving ad engine that does the heavy lifting." |
| 0:10–0:18 | Screen-rec: brand onboarding — logo upload, AI extracting palette + tone into the Brand Playbook. | "First, it learns *your* brand. Upload your logo, and AdForge builds a playbook — your colors, your voice, your rules." |
| 0:18–0:28 | The 4-step wizard: goal → audience → offer → style. AI personas appear. Batch-size slider shows "≈ 48 credits · ready in ~2 min." | "Then describe one campaign. Pick your platforms. AdForge generates a *whole batch* of on-brand image and video ads — and tells you the cost before you spend a credit." |
| 0:28–0:38 | Live batch review grid filling in; reviewer hits **A / R**, then types "make it warmer" and an instant before/after revision. | "Review at the speed of thought. Approve, reject, or tell the AI exactly what to change — and watch it revise in seconds." |
| 0:38–0:46 | Token Meter page: credit gauge, by-model bars. Then the Learn page rules. | "Track every credit in real time. And the more you approve, the smarter your next batch gets." |
| 0:46–0:54 | Geek Mode: model chips, a prompt template with `{{brand.colorHexes}}` highlighted. | "Power user? Geek Mode lets you swap models and rewrite the prompts behind every step." |
| 0:54–1:00 | Logo lockup on gradient, URL + CTA button. | "AdForge AI. From brief to batch — on brand, every time. Start free today." |

**End card text:** `AdForge AI — Self-improving batch ad generation` · *Try it free →*

## 2. Instagram / TikTok Reel — "40 Ads in 4 Minutes" (~20s, 9:16)

**Style:** punchy, captions burned in, trending upbeat track. Each line is
one hard cut.

- **0:00** *(on-screen text, big):* "POV: marketing wants 40 ads by Friday 😮‍💨"
- **0:02** *(cut to app):* "So I opened AdForge AI."
- **0:04** "It already knows my brand 🎨" — *(quick flash of Brand Playbook: palette + tone)*
- **0:07** "One brief →" — *(wizard, finger taps through goal / audience / offer)*
- **0:10** "→ a whole batch 🤯" — *(grid of ads populating, both images AND a video ad playing)*
- **0:13** "Don't like one? Just tell it." — *(type "more energy" → before/after swap)*
- **0:16** "And it shows the cost up front 💸" — *(flash of ⚡ "≈ 48 credits" estimate)*
- **0:18** "Friday? Done by lunch. ✅"
- **0:20** *(end card):* "AdForge AI 🔗 link in bio — start free"

**Caption:**
> Batch ad creation that actually stays on brand. Brief in, 40 ads out, fully editable. 🚀 #AdForgeAI #AImarketing #adcreative #marketingtools

**Hook variants to A/B test (first 2s is everything):**
1. "Marketing asked for 40 ads. I made them in 4 minutes."
2. "This AI makes ads that actually look like *my* brand."
3. "Stop paying $$$ per ad. Watch this."

## Production notes

- **Capture real footage.** Every beat above maps to a real screen in the
  app (dashboard, onboarding, wizard, batch review, Token Meter, Geek Mode).
  Record at 2x scale for crisp 1080p+ exports.
- **Brand tokens:** Deep Indigo `#3525cd` → Vibrant Purple `#831ada`
  gradient; fonts Inter (body) + JetBrains Mono (labels/numbers).
- **Music:** upbeat, building — drop the beat on the "→ a whole batch" reveal.
- **Captions:** burn in for the Reel (sound-off autoplay); optional for YT.
- **CTAs:** YouTube → end card + pinned comment link. Reel → "link in bio."
